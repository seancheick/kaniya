# Kaniya — Sourcing Playbook

Ops doc for packing runs. Rule: **every box = 3 comfort/gentle · 3 protein/fiber · 2 hydration · 2 sweet · 2 savory (12 items)**. Rotate items within each category so a second box never feels like a rerun. Allergy answers from the quiz swap items 1:1 within the same category. Keep ALL items sealed in manufacturer packaging (allergen labels must stay visible). Generic names on the website; brand names live here only.

## Stores (in order for MVP)
1. **Trader Joe's** — best value/feel for curation runs
2. **Target** — easiest repeatable restock
3. **Whole Foods** — premium discovery
4. **Costco** — bulk economics on repeat items
5. **Thrive Market** — clean-label online restock
6. **Brand sites / Faire** — once volume justifies wholesale (Faire before UNFI/KeHE)

## Pregnancy Comfort pool (pick 12 by category)

**Comfort / gentle (pick 3):** Prince of Peace ginger chews · Gin Gins ginger chews · Dramamine ginger soft chews · Pink Stork nausea sweets · The Ginger People ginger lozenges · Simple Mills almond-flour crackers · saltines · pretzels · applesauce pouches · rice crackers

**Protein / fiber (pick 3):** RXBAR · KIND protein bar · Justin's almond butter packets · peanut butter packets · Biena / Saffron Road roasted chickpeas · trail mix · unsalted almonds · pistachios · roasted edamame

**Hydration (pick 2):** Liquid I.V. · Nuun tablets · coconut water carton · ginger tea · peppermint tea · lemon-ginger tea (LMNT only case-by-case — sodium runs high)

**Sweet (pick 2):** freeze-dried strawberries · dried mango · dried apricots · fruit leather · That's It bars · dark chocolate almonds · Unreal peanut butter cups · SkinnyDipped almonds · MadeGood granola minis · Simple Mills cookies · yogurt pretzels

**Savory (pick 2):** SkinnyPop / LesserEvil popcorn · pretzels · Hippeas puffs · baked cheese crackers · rice crackers

**Never in pregnancy boxes:** supplement powders, CBD, detox teas, high-caffeine items, herbal blends with unclear pregnancy safety, unpasteurized anything, alcohol-flavored products, Perfect Bars (needs refrigeration).

## Balanced Blood Sugar pool (pick 12 by category)

**Protein-forward (pick 3+):** Chomps turkey/beef sticks · Whisps · Moon Cheese · Epic bars · roasted edamame · nut butter packets · RXBAR (lower-sugar flavors only)

**Fiber / slow-carb crunch (pick 3):** Biena / The Good Bean chickpeas · roasted fava beans · Hippeas · LesserEvil or SkinnyPop single-serves · Simple Mills crackers · Mary's Gone Crackers · Flackers

**Hydration (pick 2):** Liquid I.V. Sugar-Free · Nuun Sport · Ultima Replenisher

**Lower-sugar sweet (pick 2):** Lily's dark chocolate · SkinnyDipped almonds · Unreal dark chocolate coconut · freeze-dried berries · apple chips (portion-controlled) — ChocZero sparingly (sugar alcohols bother some people)

**Nuts/seeds (fills protein or savory):** unsalted almonds · pistachios · walnuts · pumpkin seeds · sunflower seeds · chia squeeze packs

**Never in blood-sugar boxes:** regular candy, sugary granola, juice, dried-fruit-heavy mixes, honey-roasted nuts as a headline item, big naked-carb snacks.

## Pricing guardrails
12-item box at $47 → target item COGS ≤ $28 retail-sourced (validation batches run thin — that's paid research), ≤ $18 once wholesale. Tiers later: Mini 8 items $29–32 · Deluxe 16–18 items $69–79.
